Primary annotation levels

Read the evidence guide

Each TF sequence record has one primary annotation selected by a hierarchy. All supporting motif and model evidence remains visible on the TF page.

Direct/public PWM

A PWM linked directly to this UniProt sequence record from a public motif collection.

Internal label: Identical_PWM

Close homologous PWM

A PWM transferred from a close homolog.

Internal label: Homologous_PWM

Distant homologous candidate

A candidate PWM transferred from a more distant homolog.

Internal label: Relatively_Homologous_PWM

ModCRE predicted motif

A motif predicted from a precomputed protein-DNA structural model.

Internal label: ModCRE

AlphaFold3-assisted ModCRE

A motif predicted using an AlphaFold3-assisted structural model.

Internal label: AlphaFold

No primary motif selected

No primary annotation was selected in the final hierarchy.

Internal label: Unannotated
