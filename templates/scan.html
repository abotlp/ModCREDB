PWM scanner

Scan DNA sequence

This scanner uses MEME/FIMO to search pasted DNA or FASTA sequences with selected PWMs. FIMO reports statistical p-values and q-values for predicted binding sites.

What to put in the scan

Choose one or more motifs, paste a DNA sequence, keep 1e-4 for a strict first scan, then press Scan. A larger p-value such as 0.01 is more permissive and may return weaker hits.

Fill TEAD1 example: scan a short sequence with the direct TEAD1 motif set linked to UniProt P28347. It illustrates candidate TEAD-like motif matches, not a confirmed binding site.
