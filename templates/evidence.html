Practical interpretation

QuestionRecommended answer
Which motif should I use first?Prefer Known motifs, then Nearest Neighbor (>70%), then Nearest Neighbor (70% - 40%), then Predicted ModCRE/AlphaFold-assisted motifs.
Are predicted motifs low quality?No. The public label should be Predicted, not “low”. They are structure-derived hypotheses and should be inspected with their region, template, and model support.
Why do some motifs have identity percentages?The percentage describes similarity to the TF used to transfer the PWM. Known motifs do not need a transfer percentage.
Why are some referenced motifs missing?Some archive records do not contain a generated PWM and are labeled Missing MEME in public views.
