Practical interpretation

Question Recommended answer
Which motif should I trust first? Prefer identical known motifs, then close homologs, then more distant homologs, then predicted ModCRE and AlphaFold3-assisted ModCRE motifs.
Can predicted motifs be useful? Yes. They are valuable when no experimental motif exists, but they should be treated as hypotheses.
Why do some motifs have identity percentages? The percentage describes similarity to the TF used to transfer the PWM. Direct identical motifs do not need a transfer percentage.
Why are some referenced motifs missing? The TSV appears to include a few JASPAR motif IDs from older or mixed releases. They are kept as references and reported in internal QC/data-quality notes.
