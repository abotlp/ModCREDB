What Users Can Do

  • Search TFs by UniProt accession, gene/protein annotation, PFAM family/domain, legacy family tag, or motif ID.
  • Inspect PWM sequence logos and download MEME motif files.
  • View linked 3D models when model files are available.
  • Scan pasted DNA sequences using selected motifs or all generated PWMs linked to a TF.
  • Inspect model-supported DNA-binding regions and overlapping PFAM domains.

Important Interpretation

PWM prediction and 3D-model support are related but not identical. Many TFs have Known or nearest-neighbor motifs without a local structural model. Some model records support regions even when a particular motif file lacks a generated PWM.

The public label for structure-derived motifs is Predicted, not “low”. Use region, template, identity, and model support to interpret them.
